Are you aware that we are using up Mother Earth's resources at an amazing pace?


A lot of us buy things that we only need to use once. And then, we just keep it aside, or throw them away. Beside wasting precious resources, it is also a waste of $$.


Now, we can do our little bit for the future generations.

A friend in need allows you to post requests for items that you will like to borrow. And also items that you have little use at the moment.

Some scenarios will be:

  • Going for a winter holiday from Singapore? You can borrow a winter jacket instead of buying one purely for this trip

  • Got a new Xbox Kinect? You can borrow games from your friend to try them out first

  • Planning a camping trip? You can borrow the tent and other equipment from a friend
